Satisfaction Guarantee

At Scoop Force, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality service. If you’re not satisfied with a cleaning, let us know within 24 hours, and we’ll return to fix the issue at no additional cost.

Refund Eligibility

  • One-Time Cleanings:
    • 48hrs notice: Full refund
    • 24hrs notice: 50% refund
    • Less than 24hrs: No refund, $10 rescheduling fee applies.
  • Recurring Subscriptions: Prepaid services are non-refundable but will be honored through the remainder of the billing period.
  • Overpayment or Billing Errors: Refunds will be issued promptly for any errors.

How to Cancel or Request a Refund

Email us at ScoopForceCleaners@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Your name
  • Service address
  • Scheduled service date
  • Reason for cancellation or refund request

Exceptions

Severe weather may result in rescheduling rather than cancellation. There are no additional fees for weather-related rescheduling.

Why We Charge an Overgrown Yard Fee

An overgrown yard takes significantly more time to clean due to tall grass, dense weeds, or debris, which makes navigation and waste removal more challenging.

Fee Structure

  • Mild Overgrowth: Grass 6–12 inches, minimal weeds. Fee: $15 per visit.
  • Moderate Overgrowth: Grass 12–18 inches, moderate weeds, or slight debris. Fee: $25 per visit.
  • Severe Overgrowth: Grass over 18 inches, dense weeds, or significant debris. Fee: $40–$60 per visit.

Assessment

We will assess your yard’s condition during the first visit and provide a clear explanation and fee estimate before beginning work.

How It Works

Keeping Your Pets Safe

At Scoop Force, we prioritize the safety and well-being of your pets during every visit.

Policy Details

  • Securing Pets: Pets must be secured indoors or in a designated area during service to ensure safety.
  • Notification of Behavior: Inform us of any territorial or aggressive behavior when scheduling service.
  • Fences and Gates: Ensure all gates and fences are secure to prevent accidental escapes.
  • Unforeseen Circumstances: If a pet is unsecured or aggressive, we may reschedule the service.
